Registretion Info

To register for the tournament, please fill out the registration form available on our website. The form will require you to provide your team name, player names, contact information, and any relevant details about your team. Once you have submitted the form, you will be notified when you are going to play in 2 weeks

Rules

Here are the rules for the tournament:

  • Teams must consist of two players.
  • Matches will be played in a best-of-three sets format up to 21 points.
  • Any disputes will be resolved by the tournament officials.
  • For the Men's and Women's categories, the rules of official EOPE handbook will apply
