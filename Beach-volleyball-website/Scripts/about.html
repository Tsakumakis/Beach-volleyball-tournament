About us

We are a small team that is trying to "revive" volleyball in Corfu. By doing this tournament this enhances the volleyball comunity of our island and hopefully make new players fall in love with the sport.

Registretion Info

To register for the tournament, please fill out the registration form available on our website. The form will require you to provide your team name, player names, contact information, and any relevant details about your team. Once you have submitted the form, you will be notified when you are going to play in 2 weeks. It is also important to tell us if you played in an indoor team or have participated in a tournament in the past

Advice

Our Co-ed Division is all about having a great time and meeting new people. We’re specifically looking for more guys to join the mix—so if you’ve been hesitant because you’re not a 'pro' yet, this is the spot for you.

Important

We strongly advice you to come 30 minutes earlier that your sceduled match so we can be sure that you are there and can warmup properly

Rules

Here are the rules for the tournament:

  • Teams must consist of two players. (No substitute)
  • Matches will be played in a best-of-three sets format up to 21 points.
  • Any disputes will be resolved by the tournament officials.
  • For the Men's and Women's categories, the rules of official EOPE handbook will apply

Co'ed Rules:

  • Teams must consist of two players (two men two women)
  • Matches will be played in a best-of-three sets format up to 21 points.
  • No limitation play, play however you want
  • The same rules as Men's and Women's categories will be applied

Place and time

the tournament will be during the 23-24th of may (23 will be the coed and 24 the Men's and Women's category). The time of the matches will be from 9am to 5pm.
